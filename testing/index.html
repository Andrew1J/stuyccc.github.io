Competitive Programming

At StuyCCC, you'll enter the world of competitive programming.

Time Limits

Instead of spending weeks on a single project, you'll have 2-3 hours to efficiently solve a set of problems.

Constraints

Every problem comes with well-defined input and output - you'll never have to worry about unintended behavior.

Algorithms

Problems draw on a myriad of complex algorithms that extend far beyond what Stuy CS classes go over.
