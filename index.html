PClassic

The PClassic competition takes place virtually on November 20th. It takes place from 10AM - 5PM, and we will take the competition in the Stuyvesant building.

Teams can have anywhere from 1-4 members, so you can enter your team at this google form if you have already decided on one. If you do not yet have a team, you can go to our discord to communicate with other members before deciding on a team. We also have a list of our members on StuyActivities, if you want to find people to ask.

In order to participate in this competition, you'll need one person to make a CodeChef account for the team. Please submit your CodeChef username after creating your account. You can find the CodeChef platform here.

There will be two division: Novice and Standard. If your team is composed of people new to CS, we'd recommend choosing the novice division. If you have experience coding and are up for a challenge, we'd recommend participating in the standard division.

The only two languages you are allowed to compete in are Python and Java.

The PClassic website is here. You can find the rules, as well as past problem sets to practice / gauge difficulty, at their website.

The Google Form to sign up is due at 11:59:59 on Friday, November 12th, but it is suggested that you submit earlier if you can.
