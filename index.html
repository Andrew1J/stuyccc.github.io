PClassic

PClassic (one of our main competitions that we try to attend) will be happening on Saturday Nov 20th at the Stuyvesant building. It is a team competition with up to 4 members per team, so find some buddies who want to program with you. It is a bit on the difficult side, but we encourage everyone to try. If you want to get a taste of their difficulty, you can look at past contests on their website. We will be sending a sign up form and more information soon, so be on the lookout for that.
